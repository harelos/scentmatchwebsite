How ScentSnap Works

From installation to your first money-saving dupe discovery — ScentSnap takes less than 60 seconds to set up, and zero effort to use.

1
Step One
Install the Extension
Head to the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome." The extension installs in one click. No account required, no email, no payment — just click and you're done. ScentSnap appears as a small icon in your Chrome toolbar.
🔒 Privacy note: We never ask for personal information. Ever.
2
Step Two
Browse Your Favorite Retailers
Visit Amazon, Sephora, Ulta, FragranceNet, Nordstrom, Macy's, Fragrantica, or any other supported site. No special steps needed — simply browse as you normally would. ScentSnap silently watches for perfume pages in the background.
🛍️ 8+ supported retailers and growing every month.
3
Step Three
ScentSnap Activates Automatically
When you land on a perfume product page, ScentSnap automatically detects the fragrance name and cross-references our built-in database. Within a second, a panel appears showing the perfume's complete scent profile in plain English — no bizarre marketing poetry, just clear descriptions like "warm vanilla, dry sandalwood, touch of citrus."
⚡ Instant: No clicks. No loading. Just automatic intelligence.
4
Step Four
Discover Dupes & Save
Below the scent profile, ScentSnap lists the best matching affordable alternatives — each with a match percentage, price comparison, and direct Amazon shopping link. The savings are displayed prominently so you know exactly how much you're keeping. The choice is always yours, but now it's an informed one.
💸 Users save an average of $240 per purchase decision.
