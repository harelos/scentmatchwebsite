The Secret

Why Dupes Actually Work

High-end fragrances cost so much because of brand prestige, bottle design, and marketing — not because the scent formula is truly unique. Many budget brands use nearly identical ingredient combinations.

The fragrance community has spent years cataloguing these similarities. ScentSnap packages this collective knowledge into instant browser-level intelligence that activates wherever you shop.

Every dupe recommendation in ScentSnap is based on shared scent notes, scent family classification, and community consensus — not guesswork. Match percentages reflect how similar the scent profiles are, not an exact chemical guarantee.

Transparency

Our Dupe Methodology

We believe in being upfront about how our matching system works.

How we build dupe matches: Our database is curated from fragrance community research, expert reviews on platforms like Fragrantica and Basenotes, and shared ingredient analysis. Match percentages are estimates based on shared scent notes, scent family classification, and community consensus.

What match percentages mean: A 90% match means the budget fragrance shares a very similar scent profile — the same dominant notes, similar progression, and comparable character. It does not mean the formulas are identical. Scent is subjective; personal experience may vary.
