Feature 01
Scent DNA Decoder
Perfume brand descriptions are notoriously useless. "A luminous cascade of solar radiance" tells you absolutely nothing about how something smells.
ScentSnap translates every fragrance into plain English — the actual notes, the mood, the wearability — based on community consensus and fragrance expert analysis.
  • Top, heart, and base note breakdown
  • Plain English descriptions ("warm vanilla + dry wood + light citrus")
  • Scent family classification (floral, woody, oriental, fresh, etc.)
  • Season and occasion guidance

Feature 02
Longevity & Sillage Ratings
Two of the most important buying factors — and two things brands almost never disclose honestly. ScentSnap shows you community-tested ratings for how long a perfume lasts on skin and how far it projects.
  • Longevity: 0-10 scale (0 = fades in 1hr, 10 = lasts 12+ hours)
  • Sillage: Intimate / Moderate / Strong / Beast-mode
  • Based on aggregated community reviews, not brand marketing
  • Helps you choose for the right occasion

Feature 03
Privacy-First Design
ScentSnap was built with privacy as a first principle, not an afterthought. The entire fragrance database is bundled inside the extension — meaning it works completely offline and never needs to contact any server for fragrance data.
  • No browsing history collected or transmitted
  • No personal identifiers stored anywhere
  • Offline database — no network requests for data lookups
  • Storage permission used only for your local preferences
  • activeTab permission used only on supported sites, only while active
What We Do NOT Collect
  • ✓Browsing history or URLs visited
  • ✓Name, email, or any personal data
  • ✓Purchase history or behavior
  • ✓Cookies or user identifiers
  • ✓Search queries or form inputs
  • ✓Device or location information
